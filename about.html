About Me

Hi, I'm Pamela!

Originally from upstate NY, I'm currently located outside the Chattanooga, TN area.

I'm always up for the chance to experience new cultures and try new things. Some of my most memorable experiences have been backpacking and paragliding in the Andes Mountains, night kayaking on Puerto Rico's bioluminescent bay, touring castles in Spain, and standing on the equator in Ecuador.

I love international cuisine and learning new languages. In my free time, I'm either out mountain biking, hiking, volunteering, or hanging out with friends.
